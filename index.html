    The Smart thru axle receives the wireless signal to carry out the shifting process electronically.     ROSE BIKES

    ROSE Bikes, is a renowned German bicycle brand celebrated for its high-quality craftsmanship and innovative designs. Specializing in road, gravel, mountain, and urban bikes, they offer a wide range of models to suit all cycling needs. Their direct-to-consumer approach allows customers to fully customize their bikes online, from frame colors to components. Known for their lightweight aluminum and carbon frames, ROSE bikes deliver excellent performance and durability. Popular models like the Backroad, X-Lite, and Thrill Hill have gained global recognition. The brand emphasizes cutting-edge technology and sustainability in production. Competitive pricing makes their bikes accessible without compromising quality. With a focus on precision and design, ROSE Bikes caters to both amateurs and professionals. Their sleek aesthetics and German engineering ensure a premium riding experience.
